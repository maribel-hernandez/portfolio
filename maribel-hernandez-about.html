Los Angeles native, Maribel Hernandez, is a first-generation Latina who comes from a low-income family and is currently in pursue of her Associates Degree in Graphic Design at Santa Monica College. In 2018, Hernandez received her Bachelor's of Science degree in Business Administration, Marketing concentration from the University of La Verne.

Post-graduation, Hernandez was offered a contract with Univision's Multi Channel Network (MCN), as a Temp Campaign Manager. Although, the experience was incredible, Hernandez soon realized that her true passion and calling was graphic design. Hernandez decided to risk the offer of renewing her contract with Univision and purse her passion for design. Since then, Hernandez has established herself as a freelance graphic designer with a keen eye for layout, composition, and detail in order to ensure a flawless execution.
